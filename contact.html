We are the modern medical clinic in the centre of Boston

Our clinic based in Bedford, NH, is a federally qualified health center that has been offering high-quality, comprehensive, and family-oriented primary health care and support services since March 7th, 1935. We are recognized by the National Committee on Quality Assurance as a Patient-Centered Medical Home. We provide health care for a stronger community by serving nearly 20,000 patients annually.
